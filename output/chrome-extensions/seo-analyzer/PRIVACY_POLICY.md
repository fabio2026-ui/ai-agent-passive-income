# Privacy Policy - SEO Analyzer Extension

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## Overview
SEO Analyzer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We take a privacy-first approach. SEO Analyzer does NOT:

- ❌ Collect personal information
- ❌ Track your browsing history
- ❌ Send data to external servers
- ❌ Use analytics or tracking tools
- ❌ Require account registration
- ❌ Store data in the cloud

## Information Processing

All analysis performed by SEO Analyzer happens locally on your device:

- ✅ Page content is analyzed in your browser
- ✅ No data leaves your computer
- ✅ Results are stored only in your browser's local storage
- ✅ You can clear all data by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions Explanation

Our extension requests the following permissions:

**activeTab**: Required to analyze the webpage you're currently viewing
**storage**: Required to save your preferences locally
**host_permissions**: Required to access page content for SEO analysis

## Third-Party Services

SEO Analyzer does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs.

## Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device:
- No server breaches are possible
- No data transmission means no interception risk
- You maintain full control of your information

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the extension and updating the "Last updated" date.
