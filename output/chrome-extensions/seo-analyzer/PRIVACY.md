# SEO Analyzer Extension

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

### Overview
SEO Analyzer is a browser extension that helps users analyze website SEO elements. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

### Data Collection

**What we collect:**
- ✅ **Extension settings** (stored locally in your browser)
- ✅ **Website metadata** (title, description, headings - analyzed in real-time)

**What we DON'T collect:**
- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Login credentials
- ❌ Any data is NOT sent to external servers

### Data Usage

All website analysis is performed **locally** in your browser. No data is transmitted to any server.

### Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

### Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
