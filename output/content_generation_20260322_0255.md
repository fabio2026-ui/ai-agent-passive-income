Your personal breathing coach powered by heart rate variability (HRV) — reduce stress and improve sleep in 2 minutes.

（你的个人呼吸教练，基于心率变异性(HRV)技术 — 2分钟减压助眠。）

---

### 详细描述

**The Problem**

We all know breathing exercises help with stress and sleep. But generic "breathe in 4, hold 7, out 8" doesn't work for everyone. Your body is different at 9 AM vs 9 PM, after coffee vs after yoga, during a deadline vs on vacation.

**The Solution**

Breath AI reads your Apple Watch / Garmin / Fitbit HRV data and generates a personalized breathing rhythm that matches your current physiological state. No guessing. No one-size-fits-all.

**How it works:**
1. Connect your wearable (Apple Health, Garmin Connect, etc.)
2. Open Breath AI and select your goal: Focus, Sleep, or Calm
3. Follow the AI-generated breathing guide for 2-10 minutes
4. Track your HRV improvement over time

**Built for:**
- 💻 Developers who need focus between meetings
- 📚 Students dealing with exam anxiety  
- 😴 People struggling with sleep onset
- 🏢 High-pressure professionals managing chronic stress

---

### 亮点列表（Maker Comments）

🧠 **HRV-Driven Personalization** — Not pre-recorded audio. Real-time adaptation based on your heart rate variability.

⚡ **2-Minute Modes** — No "commit to 20 minutes of meditation" guilt. Quick sessions for real life.

🔒 **Privacy First** — Health data stays on your device. No cloud storage. No selling your data. Ever.

📊 **Progress Tracking** — See your HRV trends, session history, and stress patterns over weeks and months.

🎯 **Goal-Based Sessions** — Focus mode for work, Sleep mode for bedtime, Calm mode for panic moments.

🌙 **Works Offline** — No internet needed. Use it on planes, subway, or when you just want to disconnect.
