========================================

然后点击右上角头像 → Profile

添加技能: Video Editing, TikTok Marketing, Content Creation

========================================
🎯 第三步: 创建Gig
========================================

点击: Selling → Gigs → Create A New Gig

----------------------------------------
标题 - 直接复制:
----------------------------------------
I will create data-driven short videos that get millions of views using AI

类别选择:
- Category: Video & Animation
- Subcategory: Video Editing

点击 Save & Continue
