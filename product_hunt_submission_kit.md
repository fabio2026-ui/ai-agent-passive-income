## 📝 产品描述

### 简短描述 (Short Description) - 260字符以内
Breath AI is an intelligent breathing companion that uses AI to guide you through personalized breathing exercises. Reduce stress, improve sleep, and boost focus with science-backed techniques tailored to your needs.

> Breath AI是一款智能呼吸伴侣，使用AI引导你进行个性化呼吸练习。通过针对你需求定制的科学方法，减轻压力、改善睡眠、提升专注力。

### 详细描述 (Long Description)

**Breath AI** transforms the way you breathe, relax, and recharge.

🧘 **Personalized Sessions**: Our AI analyzes your breathing patterns and emotional state to create customized exercises just for you.

🌙 **Sleep Better**: Fall asleep faster with guided breathing techniques designed to calm your nervous system.

⚡ **Stress Relief**: Instant relief during anxious moments with quick 2-minute breathing resets.

📊 **Track Progress**: Monitor your breathing journey with insights and streaks.

🔊 **Immersive Audio**: Binaural beats and nature sounds enhance your practice.

**Perfect for:**
- Professionals dealing with workplace stress
- Students preparing for exams
- Anyone struggling with sleep
- Meditation beginners and enthusiasts

Join thousands already breathing better with Breath AI. Your first deep breath starts now.
