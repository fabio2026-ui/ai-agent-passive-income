```
🫁 What if you could reduce stress as effectively as meditation, but in just 2 minutes?

Breathing AI is a smart breathing coach that guides you through scientifically-proven breathing techniques used by Navy SEALs, Olympic athletes, and Silicon Valley executives.

• 4 guided modes: Relax, Focus, Sleep, Energy
• Stanford neuroscientist-backed techniques
• No signup required - works instantly
• Completely free, forever

🎯 Perfect for:
• Before meetings or presentations
• When feeling anxious or overwhelmed
• Before sleep
• Mid-day energy boost

No meditation apps. No subscriptions. Just breathe.
```

```
So I built the tool I wish I had: simple, fast, and free.

These aren't random breathing patterns. Each mode is based on research from Stanford, Harvard Medical School, and the military's tactical breathing programs.
```
