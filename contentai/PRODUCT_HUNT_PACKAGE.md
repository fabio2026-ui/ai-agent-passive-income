## 📝 发布内容

### 产品名称
ContentAI

### 一句话描述
AI content generator with zero operating costs - bring your own API key

### 详细描述
ContentAI is a zero-cost AI content generator that puts users in control. Instead of charging monthly subscriptions, we let you bring your own Moonshot AI API key.

**Key Features:**
• 5 content types: blog posts, social media, product descriptions, emails, ad copy
• No signup required - completely anonymous
• Privacy-first - nothing stored on servers
• IPFS deployed - always available
• Open source (coming soon)

### Maker评论
"I built ContentAI in a single day because I was frustrated with AI writing tools that charge $30-50/month. BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) is the perfect model for occasional users like me."
