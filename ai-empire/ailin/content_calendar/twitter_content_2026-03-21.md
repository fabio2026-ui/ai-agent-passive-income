**Time**: 11:30 AM
**Status**: Draft

VAT registration thresholds in 2025 (simplified):

🇬🇧 UK: £85,000/year
🇩🇪 Germany: €22,000 (first year) / €50,000 (subsequent)
🇫🇷 France: €85,800 (goods) / €34,400 (services)

🇪🇸 Spain: €0 (immediate registration)

🚨 Gotcha: Selling on Amazon EU?
You're deemed to have a fixed establishment.
Thresholds don't apply. Register immediately.

€12,000 in back taxes + penalties.

Know before you sell.
