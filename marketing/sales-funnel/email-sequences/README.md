## 2. Nurture Sequence (Weekly)

### Email Types (Rotate Weekly)

#### Type A: Educational Content
**Subject:** [Specific tip related to their pain point]

**Structure:**
1. Hook (problem they face)
2. Teach (actionable tip/strategy)
3. Bridge (how this relates to your solution)
4. Soft CTA (link to resource/offer)

#### Type B: Case Study/Story
**Subject:** [Before] → [After] in [Timeframe]

**Structure:**
1. The "before" situation
2. The turning point
3. The "after" results
4. CTA (learn more/book call)

#### Type C: FAQ/Objecion Handler
**Subject:** "Is this right for me if..."

**Structure:**
1. Address common objection
2. Re-frame the concern
3. Social proof/counter-example
4. CTA (questions? reply)

#### Type D: Behind-the-Scenes
**Subject:** What we're building next 🚀

**Structure:**
1. Personal update
2. What's coming
3. Early access offer
4. CTA (join waitlist/reserve spot)

## 3. Pitch Sequence (Sales Push)

### Pre-Launch (Days -3 to -1)

**Email 1 (Day -3):** Tease the offer
- Subject: "Something big is coming..."
- Hint at the problem it solves
- Build anticipation

**Email 2 (Day -2):** Reveal details
- Subject: "Opening tomorrow: [Offer Name]"
- What it is, who it's for, price
- Cart open time

**Email 3 (Day -1):** FAQ + Last Chance
- Subject: "Doors open in 24 hours"
- Address objections
- Early bird bonus reminder

### Launch (Days 0-3)

**Email 4 (Day 0):** OPEN NOW
- Subject: "🚨 [OFFER NAME] IS LIVE"
- Full details, bonuses, urgency
- Multiple CTAs

**Email 5 (Day 1):** Social Proof
- Subject: "Already [X] people joined..."
- Testimonials
- Scarcity reminder

**Email 6 (Day 2):** Deep Dive
- Subject: "Here's exactly what you get:"
- Detailed breakdown
- FAQ

**Email 7 (Day 3):** Final Call
- Subject: "⏰ Final hours: [Offer Name] closes tonight"
- Countdown timer
- Last chance bonuses

### Post-Launch (Days 4-7)

**Email 8 (Day 4):** Waitlist/Next Time
- Subject: "Missed it? Here's what to do..."
- Waitlist for next round
- Downsell offer

## Technical Setup Guide

### Email Platform Recommendations
1. **ConvertKit** - Best for creators
2. **ActiveCampaign** - Best automation
3. **Klaviyo** - Best for e-commerce
4. **Mailchimp** - Best free plan
5. **SendGrid** - Best transactional

### Automation Triggers
```
TRIGGER: User subscribes via [lead magnet]
→ ACTION: Add to "Welcome Sequence" tag
→ DELAY: 0 minutes
→ SEND: Email 1

TRIGGER: Email 1 opened
→ ACTION: Wait 24 hours
→ SEND: Email 2 (if not purchased)

TRIGGER: Cart abandoned
→ ACTION: Add to "Abandoned Cart" sequence
→ DELAY: 1 hour
→ SEND: Cart Email 1

TRIGGER: 60 days since last email open
→ ACTION: Add to "Re-engagement" sequence
```

### Segmentation Rules
- By lead source (which magnet they downloaded)
- By engagement (opens, clicks)
- By purchase history
- By behavior (visited pricing page, etc.)

### Best Practices
1. **Send times:** 10 AM or 2 PM (test your audience)
2. **Frequency:** 2-3x per week during nurture, daily during launches
3. **Subject lines:** Keep under 50 characters
4. **Mobile:** 60%+ open on mobile - keep it scannable
5. **Personalization:** Use {{first_name}} and dynamic content
6. **Testing:** A/B test subject lines and send times
