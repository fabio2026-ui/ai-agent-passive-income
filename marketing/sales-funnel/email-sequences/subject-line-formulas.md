# Email Subject Line Formulas

## High-Converting Formulas by Type

### 1. Curiosity Gap
- "The [adjective] secret to [desired outcome]"
- "What [famous person] knows about [topic] that you don't"
- "I can't believe I'm sharing this..."
- "The truth about [common belief]"
- "[Number] things nobody tells you about [topic]"

**Example:** "The weird trick that 3x'd our email open rates"

---

### 2. Urgency/Scarcity
- "⏰ [Number] hours left: [offer]"
- "Only [number] spots left for [event]"
- "Last call: [offer] ends tonight"
- "This expires at midnight"
- "⚠️ Your access expires in [timeframe]"

**Example:** "⏰ 4 hours left: 50% off ends at midnight"

---

### 3. Personalization
- "{{first_name}}, quick question..."
- "{{first_name}}, this is for you"
- "Is this you, {{first_name}}?"
- "{{first_name}}, I noticed you [action]"
- "Can I ask you something, {{first_name}}?"

**Example:** "Sarah, this made me think of you"

---

### 4. How-To/Educational
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "The [number]-step process to [outcome]"
- "How [famous person] [achieves result]"
- "The beginner's guide to [topic]"
- "How I [achieved result] (step-by-step)"

**Example:** "How to write emails that sell in 15 minutes"

---

### 5. Numbers/Lists
- "[Number] ways to [achieve result]"
- "[Number] [topic] mistakes you're making"
- "[Number] [resources] I wish I knew about sooner"
- "[Number] things I learned [doing something]"
- "Top [number] [topic] for [year]"

**Example:** "7 email mistakes costing you sales"

---

### 6. Question
- "Are you making this [topic] mistake?"
- "Why do [X]% of [audience] fail at [goal]?"
- "What would you do with [desirable outcome]?"
- "Is [common approach] actually hurting you?"
- "Ever wondered why [observation]?"

**Example:** "Are you still doing email marketing manually?"

---

### 7. Direct Benefit
- "Get [specific result] without [common pain]"
- "The easiest way to [achieve result]"
- "[Result] is easier than you think"
- "Want [desired outcome]? Read this."
- "Stop [pain point]. Start [benefit]."

**Example:** "Get more customers without spending more on ads"

---

### 8. Social Proof
- "How [customer] [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
- "The [topic] strategy [number] people are using"
- "Why [X]+ [audience] swear by this"
- "[Customer]'s [result] story"
- "See why [famous person] recommends this"

**Example:** "How Mike doubled his revenue in 60 days"

---

### 9. Pain Point
- "Still struggling with [problem]?"
- "Tired of [common frustration]?"
- "Frustrated by [challenge]?"
- "The real reason you can't [achieve goal]"
- "Why [common solution] isn't working for you"

**Example:** "Tired of emails that get ignored?"

---

### 10. Announcement/Update
- "New: [product/feature] is here"
- "Announcing: [event/offer]"
- "It's finally ready..."
- "🚀 [Update] just launched"
- "[Good news] for [audience]"

**Example:** "🚀 The template library is finally here"

## Best Practices

### Length
- **Optimal:** 6-10 words (41-50 characters)
- **Mobile:** Keep under 30 characters for full display
- **Desktop:** Up to 60 characters visible

### Formatting
- **Emojis:** Use sparingly, test deliverability
- **Numbers:** Digits perform better than words ("5" vs "five")
- **Personalization:** First name increases opens by 20%+
- **Question marks:** Create engagement
- **Brackets:** [Video], [Free Download] set expectations

### Avoid
- ALL CAPS (triggers spam filters)
- Multiple exclamation points!!!
- Spam words: FREE, ACT NOW, BUY NOW
- Clickbait that doesn't deliver
- Misleading subjects

## A/B Testing Framework

### Test These Elements
1. **With vs without emoji**
2. **Question vs statement**
3. **Personalized vs generic**
4. **Number vs no number**
5. **Short vs long**
6. **Curiosity vs benefit**
7. **Urgency vs evergreen**

### Testing Process
```
1. Send to 20% of list (10% A, 10% B)
2. Wait 4 hours
3. Determine winner
4. Send winner to remaining 80%
5. Document results
```

## Subject Line Analyzer Checklist

- [ ] Under 50 characters?
- [ ] No spam trigger words?
- [ ] Clear value or curiosity?
- [ ] Mobile-friendly preview?
- [ ] Matches email content?
- [ ] Emojis used strategically (max 1-2)?
- [ ] Personalization token works?
- [ ] Urgency is real (not fake)?
- [ ] First word grabs attention?
- [ ] Would YOU open this?
