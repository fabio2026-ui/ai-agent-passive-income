## 📈 Funnel Metrics Framework

### Key Performance Indicators

| Stage | Metric | Target | Tool |
|-------|--------|--------|------|
| **Traffic** | Sessions | - | GA4 |
| **Opt-in** | Conversion Rate | 30%+ | Landing page |
| **Email** | Open Rate | 25%+ | Email platform |
| **Email** | Click Rate | 5%+ | Email platform |
| **Sales** | Conversion Rate | 2-5% | Checkout |
| **ROI** | ROAS | 3x+ | GA4 + Ads |

### Conversion Benchmarks
```
Typical Sales Funnel:

1000 Visitors
  300 Leads (30% opt-in)
   75 Opens (25% of leads)
   15 Clicks (5% of leads)
    3 Sales (20% of engaged)
    
Conversion Rate: 0.3% visitor to sale
(Industry average: 0.1-1%)
```

## 🆘 Troubleshooting Guide

| Problem | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Low opt-in rate | Weak offer/headline | Test new lead magnet |
| Low email opens | Poor subject lines | A/B test subjects |
| Low click rate | Weak email copy | Add more value/CTAs |
| Low sales | Price/offer mismatch | Test pricing/payment plans |
| High bounce rate | Slow page speed | Optimize images/hosting |
