## 2️⃣ Cloudflare

### ❌ Program Status: **NO PUBLIC AFFILIATE PROGRAM**

### 🚫 Important Finding

> **Cloudflare does NOT have a public affiliate program that pays commissions to content creators.**

This is a common misconception in the affiliate marketing space. Multiple authoritative sources confirm:

- Community forums explicitly state no commission-based affiliate program exists
- Developer-focused affiliate roundups consistently exclude Cloudflare
- Only B2B partner programs are available

### 🔄 Alternative Options

#### Option A: Cloudflare Partners Program (Agency/Reseller)
```
URL: https://www.cloudflare.com/partners/
Type: B2B Services Partner
Requirements: Registered business, service capabilities
Commission: Variable based on services delivered
```

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Registered business entity
- [ ] Demonstrable technical expertise
- [ ] Managed service offerings
- [ ] Minimum revenue commitments
- [ ] Technical certification (recommended)

**Not Suitable For:**
- Individual content creators
- Bloggers without service offerings
- Those seeking passive income

#### Option B: Alternative Programs to Promote

| Alternative | Commission | Why Promote |
|-------------|------------|-------------|
| **DigitalOcean** | 10% recurring × 12 months | Similar developer audience |
| **Vultr** | Up to $100 per referral | One-time high payout |
| **Vercel** | $5/lead + 30% × 6 months | Developer-focused |
| **Namecheap** | Up to 35% per sale | Domain/hosting overlap |
